FAQ

Donors

How do I sign up?
Please go to the homepage, click the 'Create Account' button and follow the prompts.
Why should I sign up? I already donate with the Red Cross
Kudos to you! We're glad you're an active donor. Unlike the Red Cross, we interface with all bloodbanks, so you don't have to register with every single one individually.
What information will you take from me?
We will require your Name, Phone Number, Email, and Zipcode, and you can fill out additional information if you wish.
Is my information protected?
We do our best to make sure your information is handled with care. However, we are not HIPPA certified at this time so we do adhere to those standards, but we aim to get there soon.

Blood banks

Why should I use your app?
Through our application, you get access to potential donors all over the country. Rather than just your donors who have signed up with you, you now have access to a whole new pool of possible donors.
How do I reach out to possible donors?
Click the EmailAdmin tab and fill out the zipcode of the event or donation center, and the radius you want to find users in. We suggest a radius under 50 miles so that those reached are within driving distance. Then add in your subject line, and body of the email and click submit. We will handle the rest.

Hospitals

Why should I use your app?
We connect you to a whole host of bloodbanks and they have access to a vast pool of donors. Additonally you can make a request for stock replenishment and only interface with our app, rather than multiple bloodbanks.
